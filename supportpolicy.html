2. Refund & Cancellation Policy

This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may or may not be issued for services rendered by Niyan IT Solutions.

2.1 General Policy

We follow a transparent and fair refund process, but due to the nature of digital, development, and IT services, certain limitations apply.

2.2 No Refunds Under These Conditions

  • If work has already started on your project
  • If a project is delayed due to client-side delays (late content, payments, approvals)
  • If the client cancels the project mid-way
  • If the client changes requirements after project commencement
  • If the final delivery was made as per the agreed scope
  • For third-party services (domains, hosting, SSL, emails, cloud, APIs)

2.3 Eligible for Partial / Conditional Refunds

  • If the project has not been started yet
  • If initial planning has been done but no development started (minus administrative charges)
  • If Niyan IT Solutions is unable to deliver as per the documented scope

2.4 Payment Terms

  • Advance payments are non-refundable
  • Milestone payments must be cleared before next phase begins
  • Late payments may affect delivery timelines

2.5 Subscription / Monthly Maintenance Plans

  • Monthly plans are non-refundable once the billing cycle starts
  • Annual plans: No refunds after 30 days of activation
  • Cancellation requests must be emailed to support@niyanit.com

2.6 Project Cancellation Process
